## About This Portfolio

Three professional economic analysis models built for Canadian
perishable farming operations. Each model is fully dynamic —
changing one assumption updates every linked sheet automatically.

Built to demonstrate readiness for an Agricultural Economic
Analyst role in Canada's perishable farming sector.

---

## Projects

### 1.  Supply Chain Cost Sheet
**Route:** Ontario greenhouse tomato farm → Toronto wholesale market

- 8 professional sheets | 103 live Excel formulas
- 6 cost categories: Farm Inputs, Harvest & Pack, Cold Chain,
  Logistics, Spoilage, Administration
- Scenario analysis across 4 market conditions
- Gross margin and selling price sensitivity

📁 [View Model](./01-supply-chain-cost-sheet/)

---

### 2.  Farm Financial Budget Model
**Operation:** 12-hectare Ontario greenhouse tomato farm (2025)

- 6 sheets | 551 live Excel formulas
- Enterprise budget (per hectare + whole farm)
- 12-month cash flow with opening/closing balances
- Break-even analysis + 5×5 price/yield sensitivity table
- Partial budget (greenhouse expansion decision)
- 5-year financial projection with CAGR

📁 [View Model](./02-farm-financial-budget/)

---

### 3.  Commodity Price Forecasting Model
**Commodities:** Fresh Tomatoes, Peppers, Lettuce (2022–2024)

- 5 sheets | 404 live Excel formulas
- 36 months of historical Canadian wholesale price data
- Moving averages (3M, 6M, 12M), MoM & YoY analysis
- Monthly seasonal index calculations
- 3-scenario 2025 price forecast (Base / Bull / Bear)
- Price driver correlation analysis (CPI, fuel, cross-commodity)
- Executive market dashboard with risk indicators

📁 [View Model](./03-commodity-price-model/)
